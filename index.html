About

Learn about the history, nutritional infomation, and some fun facts about potatoes!
Brief History

Originating in Peru, the Incas were the first civilization to cultivate potatoes. Following the Spanish conquest of the Inca Empire, potatoes were introduced to Europe. They were eventually spread throughout the world as Europe continued to colonize.

Nutritional Information

Potatoes are carbohydates contating both a bit of protien and fiber. They are also a good source of vitamin C, B6, and potassium. They are free of fat, gluten, cholesterol and plant based, making them vegan friendly.

Fun Facts

1. Potatoes were the first vegetable to be grown in space!

2. The potato plant belong to the same family as tobacco.

3. The biggest grown potato weighted about 370 pounds!

The Irish Potato Famine

Also known as the Great Famine, the Irish Potato Famine began in 1845 due to a horrible mold infestation that decimated crops for about 7 years. Millions of Irish died of starvation and related illnesses due to the lack of crops.
